Digital Literacy Tutorials

Step-by-step guides to help you master everyday apps and digital tools

Using WhatsApp

Learn how to send messages, make calls, and share photos

Getting Started with WhatsApp

  1. Download WhatsApp from the App Store (iPhone) or Play Store (Android)
  2. Open the app and verify your phone number
  3. Set up your profile with a name and photo (optional)
  4. Find contacts who use WhatsApp from your phone contacts

Sending Messages and Making Calls

  1. Tap on a contact to open a chat
  2. Type your message in the text field at the bottom
  3. Press the paper airplane icon to send
  4. For calls, tap the phone icon (voice) or video icon (video call)

Sharing Photos and Media

  1. In a chat, tap the + or paperclip icon
  2. Select Camera or Gallery/Photos
  3. Choose the image you want to share
  4. Add a caption if desired, then send

Using Paytm

Learn how to make digital payments and money transfers

Getting Started with Paytm

  1. Download Paytm from the App Store or Play Store
  2. Register using your mobile number
  3. Complete KYC verification for full access
  4. Link your bank account or add money to Paytm wallet

Making Payments

  1. Tap "Pay" on the home screen
  2. Scan QR code or enter mobile number/UPI ID
  3. Enter amount to pay
  4. Confirm payment with PIN or biometric authentication

Checking Transaction History

  1. Go to the Paytm home screen
  2. Tap "Passbook" or "Transaction History"
  3. View all your recent transactions
  4. Tap on any transaction for more details

Using Google Maps

Learn how to navigate and find places using Google Maps

Getting Started with Google Maps

  1. Open Google Maps app on your phone
  2. Allow location access when prompted
  3. The blue dot shows your current location
  4. Use two fingers to zoom in/out and one finger to move the map

Finding Directions

  1. Tap the blue "Directions" button
  2. Your current location will be set as starting point
  3. Enter your destination in the "Where to?" box
  4. Choose your mode of transport (car, walking, public transport)
  5. Tap "Start" to begin navigation

Finding Nearby Places

  1. Tap the "Explore" tab at the bottom
  2. Browse categories like Restaurants, ATMs, Hospitals, etc.
  3. Tap on any place to see details, hours, reviews
  4. Use the search bar at the top for specific places

Using Gmail

Learn how to send, receive, and organize emails

Getting Started with Gmail

  1. Open the Gmail app on your phone
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. If you don't have an account, tap "Create account"
  4. Follow the prompts to set up your new email address

Sending an Email

  1. Tap the compose button (+ or pencil icon)
  2. Enter recipient's email address in "To" field
  3. Add a subject and write your message
  4. Tap the paper airplane icon to send

Reading and Replying to Emails

  1. Tap on any email in your inbox to open it
  2. To reply, tap the reply arrow at the bottom
  3. Type your response and tap send
  4. To forward, tap the forward arrow instead

Using UPI Payments

Learn how to use UPI for quick money transfers

Understanding UPI

  1. UPI (Unified Payments Interface) connects your bank account directly to payment apps
  2. You can use apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or BHIM
  3. Money moves directly between bank accounts without wallet balance
  4. It's secure, instant, and works 24/7

Setting Up UPI

  1. Download a UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.)
  2. Register with your mobile number (should be linked to your bank)
  3. Select your bank from the list
  4. Create a UPI PIN for secure transactions

Making UPI Payments

  1. Open your UPI app
  2. Choose "Pay" or "Send Money"
  3. Enter mobile number/UPI ID or scan QR code
  4. Enter amount and note (optional)
  5. Confirm with UPI PIN

Video Calling

Learn how to make video calls with different apps

Making Video Calls with WhatsApp

  1. Open WhatsApp and go to your chat with the person
  2. Tap the video camera icon in the top right corner
  3. Wait for the other person to answer
  4. To end the call, tap the red phone icon

Using Google Meet

  1. Download and open the Google Meet app
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Tap "New meeting" or enter a meeting code
  4. Tap "Join meeting" to start or join a call

Tips for Better Video Calls

  1. Find a quiet place with good lighting
  2. Connect to WiFi for better quality
  3. Position your phone so the camera is at eye level
  4. Mute yourself when not speaking in group calls
